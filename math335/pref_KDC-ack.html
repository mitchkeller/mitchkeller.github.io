Preface Acknowledgements (KDC)

The primary source for these notes 1  is W. Ted Mahavier's notes 2  based on notes his father (W. S. Mahavier) created. I have also relied some on the notes for M361K at the University of Texas, especially for supplemental material. Frank Morgan's real analysis texts also gave me much inspiration, and various other IBL resources at www.jiblm.org and elsewhere added ideas.

Now published: Journal of Inquiry-Based Learning in Mathematics, No. 46 (2017)
Journal of Inquiry-Based Learning in Mathematics, No. 12 (2009)

Michael Spivak's Calculus lies at one further remove; it was as a TA for a course using this textbook that I first realized I understood a little analysis, and that undergraduates could actually do proofs of this type. Any errors that remain are my responsibility, of course — and if you find one, you will be suitably rewarded.

Karl-Dieter Crisman
