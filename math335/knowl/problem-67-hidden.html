Let \(f(x)=0\) for each number \(x\) in \([0,1]\) except \(x=0\text{,}\) and let \(f(0)=1\text{.}\) Show that \(U_{P}(f)>0\) for all partitions of \([0,1]\text{,}\) but that for any positive number \(\epsilon\) there is a partition \(P_\epsilon\) such that \(U_{P_\epsilon}(f)\lt \epsilon\text{.}\) In addition, fully describe all lower sums of \(f\) on \([0,1]\text{.}\)
