An Exercise or Problem is generally not an item available for the class journal, but it should usually be very helpful in understanding the content (and hopefully proofs) of the theorems. These items will generally be instructions for trying to create your own examples or open-ended investigation meant to lead you toward making a conjecture or better understanding definitions. Occasionally a problem will ask you to “prove or disprove” or “prove or disprove and salvage”. Some of these, if stated as a theorem or counterexample and theorem, may qualify for the class journal. Please ask if you have one of these that you would like to submit for publication. I will let you know if I think it qualifies.
