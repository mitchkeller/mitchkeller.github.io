I have made a somewhat significant stylistic deviation from Prof. Crisman's notes. Whereas his notes consist of a sequence of tasks labelled as “Problem” or “Example” with the Problem items being the core theoretical results to prove stated in a “Show that…” form, I have chosen to use labels such as “Theorem” and “Exercise” and to state theorem-like results in a standard implication form rather than the imperative.
