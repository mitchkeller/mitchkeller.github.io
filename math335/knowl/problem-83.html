Problem 5.37.

Let \(U\) be defined such that for each \(x\in [a,b]\text{,}\) \(U(x) = \overline{\int_a^x f}\text{,}\) and let \(L\) be defined likewise but with \(\underline{\int_a^x f}\text{.}\) Show that if \(f\) is continuous on \([a,b]\text{,}\) then \(U\) and \(L\) are differentiable, with \(U'(x)=f(x)=L'(x)\text{.}\)
