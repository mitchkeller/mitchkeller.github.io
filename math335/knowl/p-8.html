A Theorem, Lemma, or Corollary is something to be proved. Some (such as corollaries) will be very easy, a few extremely hard. Most will be somewhere in between, to enhance your senses of confidence and accomplishment. Most class time will be hearing and responding to your proofs of these items (or working in groups on proving them); they are the core of the course. Any theorem is eligible to be submitted to the class journal for publication. Generally, if a lemma supports a theorem or a corollary follows a theorem, those would be bundled together with the theorem as a single publication. If different students present proofs of these results, they could submit a joint paper if they so desired.
